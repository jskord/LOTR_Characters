Samwise Gamgee

Samwise Gamgee is Frodo's best friend. He goes with Frodo on his adventure all the way from the Shire to Mount Doom in Mordor. He is very loyal to Frodo, and saves the ring from coming under enemy hands at several key points, including when he thinks Frodo was killed by a Giant Spider he takes the ring before Frodo is taken by orcs. He then returns the ring to Frody to help him finish the quest.
