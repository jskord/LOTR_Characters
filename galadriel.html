Lady Galadriel

Lady Galadiel is one of the greatest elfs in Middle Earth. She was one of the elves given rings of power from Sauron during the Second Age. The Fellowship of the Ring takes refuge in Lothlorien, and she offers counsil and guidance to Frodo and his companions. She leaves Middle Earth at the end of the last book to sail to the Undying Isles.
