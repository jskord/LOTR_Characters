Merry Brandybuck

Merry Brandybuck is a one of the four hobbits that become part of the Fellowship of the Ring. He and Perrigrin (Pippen) Took get kidnapped by orcs, escape and lead the tree ents to sack isengard. They then play parts in the defense of Minas Tirith, and the final diversion at the Black Gate.
