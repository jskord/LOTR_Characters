Sauruman the White

Saruman the White is one of the five Wizards in Middle Earth. He was chief of the "White Council" which was formed for all who oppose sauron. His role in opposing Sauron led him toward the study of dark magic which resulted in him betraying the council and joining Sauron, in hopes of ultimately ruling Middle Earth himself. Saurumon instead was defeated at the battle of the Hornburg and the battle of Isengard
