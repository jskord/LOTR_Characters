Gandalf the Grey

Gandalf is one of the five wizards in Middle Earth. He recruited Bilbo Baggins to join the dwarves in taking back the lonely mountain from Smaug the Dragon. He also helped Frodo Baggins destroy the Ring of Power and defeat Sauron.

Famous Quotes

  • You shall not pass!!
  • Death is just another path. One that we all must take
  • What we have to do is decide what to do with the time that is given to us.
  • I suppose you think that was terribly clever.
