Boramir of Gondor

Boramir is the first son of the Steward of Gondor. He attends the meeding in Rivendell where it is decided that the Ring of Power must be destroyed and he joins the fellowship charged with the duty. While on this quest Boramir begins to desire the One Ring more and more, and eventually plots to steal it from Frodo. Boramir realizes this mistake and dies defending Frodo and the other hobbits from an Orc attack.
