Pippen

Perigrin Took is a hobbit that joins frodo, sam, and merry, on the adventure that takes them beyond the borders of the shire. He is a cousin of Frodo. He has a very enourmous apetite and has a tendancy to say and do things that frequently cause trouble or annoyance among the more senior and serious members of the Fellowship.
