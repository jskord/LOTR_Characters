Bilbo Baggins

Bilbo Baggins is the main character in the Hobbit. Gandalf the Grey recruits him to join "Thorin and Company" to help take back the Lonely Mountain from the dragon, Smaug. During his journey he acquires a strange ring from a creature known as Gollum, this ring allows him to become invisible, and it turns out this is the "One Ring" made by Sauron, Lord of Mordor.
