Frodo Baggins

Frodo Baggins is the main character in the Lord of the Rings Trilogy. He inherits a ring from Bilbo that turns out to be the Ring of Power which Sauron of Mordor needs to conquer Middle Earth. Frodo is charged with destroying the ring by taking it into Mordor and casting it into the firey lava of Mount Doom.
